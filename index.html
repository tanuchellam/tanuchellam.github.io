About Me

In December of 2012, a friend and I were walking across a pedestrian crossing in our turn when a pickup truck came swerving out of nowhere right us. I saw it coming, and in the split second I had, I ran back and yelled to my friend, who took a step back. But we weren't fast enough to outrun a speeding truck...

The moment of impact is a hollow, empty place where nothing happens.

They say your life flashes before your eyes in moments like this, and you see it what what it is, what it could be and what you want it to be. That only comes much later, but it does happen.

I am a survivor. Many months after the accident, I have so much appreciation for life and strongly believe that we should reach for our dreams - life is ephemeral and I want to do my best in giving back to this world while I am here.

In my spare time, I work with some likeminded people on a tech and strategy consulting non-profit organization called Development Solutions Organization as an Engagement Lead and help find us potential clients to work with and onboard.

I have a passion for working with people to deliver solutions in the most efficient and effective way we can to go forward. Coming from a development background, I have done some business analysis work and some project management internships before venturing into the word of strategy, mitigating risk and improving efficiency. I bring to the table a myriad of skill sets I have acquired over the years, and am enthusiastic about innovation.
