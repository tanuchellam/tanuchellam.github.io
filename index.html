This is my story

A Little About Me

Technology leader with experience managing both people and products, with a customer-first design philosophy and technical background.

In 2012, a friend and I were crossing the road, when a truck crashed into us.

It changed my life forever.

So every day, I wake up wanting to make a difference. To me, meaning comes from giving back to society in a sustainable way while being able to provide for myself and my family. Technology is a tool, and a means for impacting change, and I believe in utilizing it to solve our challenges.




Professional Life

Now UK-based, I am seeking a role where I can continue to manage and coach product managers in a fast-paced company. I am currently advising for the Harvard Business Review from London, and have recently co-founded a startup that is focused on improving training software. Prior to this, I have held managerial and leadership roles in the US and Singapore, where I have worked across corporate roles and started multiple prior companies. Most recently, at Autodesk, I headed the product team for Product Delivery, where I won the YWCA Tribute to Women award and the Autodesk CEO award in 2019 for exceptional leadership and promoting inclusive cross-functional collaboration across globally distributed teams.

MindMapper: A Side Project

Despite Mind Mapping being such a powerful tool for brainstorming, learning, note taking and presentations, there is not a good electronic Mind Mapping tool. Click to view a presentation.
