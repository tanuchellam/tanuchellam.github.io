More About Me

Product Manager, Technology Evangelist, Product Strategist, Avid Explorer, Dancer, Yoga Enthusiast...

In 2012, a friend and I were crossing the road, when a truck crashed into us.

It changed my life forever.

So every day, I wake up wanting to make a difference in my short life. To me, meaning comes from working on products that improve our lives. Technology is a tool, and a means for impacting change, and I believe in utilizing it to solve our challenges.
