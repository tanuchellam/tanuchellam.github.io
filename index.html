A Little About Me

Senior Product Manager, Founder, Advisor, Explorer, Survivor, Dancer

In 2012, a friend and I were crossing the road, when a truck crashed into us.

It changed my life forever.

So every day, I wake up wanting to make a difference in my short life. To me, meaning comes from working on products that improve our lives. Technology is a tool, and a means for impacting change, and I believe in utilizing it to solve our challenges.




Professional Life

10 years ago, I created a startup designing websites for other small and medium businesses. 5 years after that, I stopped that side-venture to help start a pro bono 501(c)(3) certified consultancy for social enterprises with some friends from Carnegie Mellon. Meanwhile, I've worked full time in product management and related roles in network security, fintech and pure tech.



Currently based out of the San Francisco Bay Area, I am now at Autodesk as a Senior Product Manager on the Product Delivery team while I help advise the non-profit organization.

Before this, I was at Barracuda Networks, where I ran product lines within Content Security. Most notably, I created and launched a new reporting product line as a platform for reporting on other Barracuda products, worked on the Essentials for Email Security product line, ran the Web Security product line and integrated new cloud micro-service projects into existing products.

You'll find me constantly trying out new products to analyze how they work, are monetized and how they can be improved. Being passionate about helping others and promote diversity in my industry, I am also a mentor with Women in Product and a contributor on Product Hunt.

MindMapper: A Side Project

Despite Mind Mapping being such a powerful tool for brainstorming, learning, note taking and presentations, there is not a good electronic Mind Mapping tool. Click to view a presentation.

We are severely lacking intuitive options to use, and the gap is that none of the graphing tools are able to convert handwriting to electronic Mind Maps accurately. This application aims to fix this gap, so the specifications are written with that in mind. Click here to view the specifications.
